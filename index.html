PROBLEM - Alternative #1

Every day people searches for news, entertainment and other kind of contents. But everyone is missing the ability to search one of the most reliable and trusted source of contents that until now wasn't accesible the same way as others: Radio.

SOLUTION

RadioCut collects, organizes and makes accesible the world radio contents.
We converted the radio from transient to permanent, in the biggest change on this century old media since its inception. This is possible now thanks to unlimited cloud storage, and it enables new ways to enjoy the tons of radio contents to the huge radio audience.
We enable the users to listen at any point of time and share pieces of radio in social networks.
Let's see how it works...
You can choose a radio station, from the country you are in, or choosing another.
Then you start listening. You can go back or forward, pause or choose a different date.
You can also see the station schedule, choose a given radio show and listen the latest emision or any other past show.
While listening, you can click the scissors and easily share a piece of radio. Just describe it with a few words and save.
Search box is available for finding stations and radio cuts. Or you can check the trending pieces of radio in your country.
We make money through ads, integrating Google’s ad networks and with partnerships with audio ads networks.
